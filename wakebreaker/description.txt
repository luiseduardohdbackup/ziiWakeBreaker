Title: Wake Breaker
Category: Game
Platform: x86 (Hybrid Graphics OpenGL ES 1.1 implementations)
Desc: A high speed water race between you and the computer through a beautiful ocean environment.  The boats pound the water behind them as they speed, through the randomly generated courses.  Each ocean environment does not only features reflections and the option of turning fog on and off, but like the race course, is different everytime you play.

Controls are as follows
UP - accelerate
RIGHT - turn right
LEFT - turn left
DOWN - reverse
NUMPAD1 - turn fog on
NUMPAD2 - turn fog off
